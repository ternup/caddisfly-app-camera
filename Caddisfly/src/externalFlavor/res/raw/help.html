IMPORTANT: Ensure that Caddisfly is correctly calibrated as per guidelines in the user manual
IMPORTANT: Ensure that Akvo Flow app is installed on the phone
INSTRUCTIONS
  1. Fill cartridge with source water
  2. Close lid and wipe cartridge dry
  3. Attach cartridge to the phone
  4. Start the Survey in Akvo Flow
  5. Press the button for the Water Quality question in the survey
  6. Wait for Caddisfly app to start
  7. Press START TEST button
  8. Shake the phone when instructed
  9. Place phone on a flat surface when instructed
  10. Wait for the test result to appear in the survey as the answer
