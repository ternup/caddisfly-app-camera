1. Fill cartridge with source water
  2. Close lid and wipe cartridge dry
  3. Attach cartridge to the phone
  4. Start a Water Quality Survey in Akvo Flow
  5. Press the button under the Water Quality question in the survey
  6. When Caddisfly is opened, press START TEST button
  7. Shake the phone when 'Shake Me' sign appears
  8. Place the phone on a flat surface when instructed
  9. Wait for test result to appear in the survey
